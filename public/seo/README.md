## Image Specifications

### Open Graph Images
- **Dimensions**: 1200x630 pixels (1.91:1 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 8MB (recommended under 1MB)
- **Content**: Should include RAC Kitengela branding and be visually appealing

### Favicon Images  
- **Formats**: ICO, PNG
- **Sizes**: 16x16, 32x32, 180x180 pixels
- **Content**: Simplified RAC Kitengela logo that's recognizable at small sizes

## Content Guidelines

### Default Site Image (`default-og.jpg`) - **HIGHEST PRIORITY**
- Main branding image for the entire RAC Kitengela organization
- Used when someone shares your main domain (rackitengela.org)
- Should include RAC Kitengela logo and key branding elements
- Represents the club's overall mission and identity
- This is the fallback image for all pages

### Homepage Image (`home.jpg`)
- Show RAC Kitengela members engaged in community service
- Include club branding/logo if possible
- Convey energy, community, and positive impact
- Should represent the club's core mission

### About Image (`about.jpg`)
- Professional team photo or leadership in action
- Show diversity and inclusivity of the club
- Convey professionalism and community focus
- Could include club meeting or planning session

### Events Image (`events.jpg`)
- Collage of various club events and activities
- Show different types of events (service, social, professional)
- Include both action shots and group photos
- Demonstrate the variety and impact of club activities

### Members Image (`members.jpg`)
- Professional group photo of current members
- Well-lit, high-quality image
- Show team unity and professionalism
- Could be from a formal club event or meeting

### Contact Image (`contact.jpg`)
- Welcoming, approachable imagery
- Could show meeting space or contact methods
- Convey accessibility and openness
- Professional but friendly tone

### Gallery Image (`gallery.jpg`)
- Collage or preview of best photos
- Show variety of club activities and moments
- High visual impact and engaging
- Representative of the full gallery content

## Technical Requirements

### File Naming
- Use lowercase letters and hyphens
- Be descriptive but concise
- Follow the naming convention in seoConfig.ts

### Optimization
- Compress images for web without losing quality
- Use tools like TinyPNG or ImageOptim
- Ensure fast loading times
- Test images on different devices and screen sizes

### Accessibility
- Include meaningful alt text for all images
- Ensure good contrast and readability
- Consider how images appear on different backgrounds
- Test with screen readers when possible
